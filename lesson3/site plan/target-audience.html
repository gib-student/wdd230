Target Audience

We hope to attract those who currently have a business here in the City of Mason, those looking to potentially start a business here, and citizens and potential citizens of the City of Mason.

Personas

  • A business owner with an open location in Mason. This person wants their busines to thrive, and they are looking for resources to help make that happen. They are looking into joining the Chamber of Commerce. They are most likely a small busines owner, and may be struggling to make ends meet. Their store is probably fairly young, but it may have been in town for quite some time as well, and they haven't joined because they never saw the need to.
  • Someone looking to open a business and they are looking for a good city to open it in. This person wants to provide the best products for the most affordable price to their customers, and so they are going to open their store in a place which will help them to do that. This person may be an entrepreneur and is putting personal assets on the line to open up this business. They are looking for a really good city friendly to business.

Scenarios

  • Current business owner with a store in Mason visits the site to find ways to help their business to improve and thrive.
  • Potential business owner looking to open up a store, and is looking to see if we have a supportive and strong Chamber of Commerce.
  • New business owner with a newly-opened store in Mason is looking to join the local Chamber of Commerce to strengthen their business.
  • A citizen of Mason is looking for local businesses which their family can visit to have enjoy some time on a weekend.
  • A government auditor is looking at this website to do some kind of audit or check.
