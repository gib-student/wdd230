Discover Mason

This is an arial view of Mason. Especially during the summertime, Mason is very beautiful with lots of green, azure skies, and cotton clouds.

The Mason Community Center is a very nice place where we have exercise equipment, basketball courts, volleyball courts, badminton nets, community and competition swimming pools, a running track, and many other community events and activites happening frequently.

The Mason Municipal Building. It houses the Mason police department, City Council meetings are held here, as well as other community meetings and activities.

The City of Mason is a well-established community, with amazing city planners and a fantastic city council.

The mascot of Mason is the Comets!

Mason is famous for its exceptional school system, and many come to live here just so their children can receive a good education. Our team mascot is the Comets!

King's Island is one of the world's foremost amusement parks, based in Mason!

Mason is home to many small businesses, as well as several well-established ones too.

Mason is a fairly small suburban town, with some country fields, many small businesses, and a few larger ones as well. Mason is becoming increasingly popular, and is starting to grow a lot.
